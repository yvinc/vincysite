## Hi! 
I’m Vincy, an undergrad at the University of British Columbia (UBC) majoring in Psychology with a minor in Data Science. I'm currently on my final Co-op round as a Learning Tech Rover with UBC's Learning Technology Hub, supporting faculty, staff, and students with educational technologies.

In summer 2025, I was a Data Science for Social Good fellow at UBC's Data Science Institute on the Mentor Canada project. Our team analyzed a national survey of over 2,800 responses to explore how mentorship shapes outcomes in mental health, education, and socioeconomic status, especially for youth from disadvantaged backgrounds.

I'm also a Teaching Assistant for STAT 201: Statistical Inference for Data Science, and I previously TA'd DSCI 100: Introduction to Data Science.

Always on a hunt for great coffee and tea. Recently fell down the rabbit hole of sourdough and pastry making despite possessing 0 talent 🧑‍🍳
